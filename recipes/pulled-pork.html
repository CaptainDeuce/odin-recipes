Slow Cooker Texas Pulled Pork

Description

This recipe consists of pulled pork with a tangy barbeque sauce on a bun. It makes around 8 sandwiches for the whole family to enjoy. The cook time is around 5 hours, so be prepared to start cooking earlier in the day.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon of vegetable oil
  • 4 pound pork shoulder roast
  • 1 cup barbeque sauce
  • 1 tablespoon yellow mustard
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 2 crushed garlic cloves
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried thyme
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 8 buttered hamburger buns
