Slow Cooker Texas Pulled Pork

Description

This recipe consists of pulled pork with a tangy barbeque sauce on a bun. It makes around 8 sandwiches for the whole family to enjoy. The cook time is around 5 hours, so be prepared to start cooking earlier in the day.

Ingredients

  • Teaspoon of vegetable oil
  • Pork shoulder
  • Barbeque sauce
  • Yellow mustard
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Apple cider vinegar
  • Chicken broth
  • Brown sugar
  • Chili powder
  • Garlic
  • Dried thyme
  • Chopped onion
  • Buttered hamburger buns
