Slush Punch

Description

This recipe makes a great punch slushie for all ages to enjoy. It takes 8 hours to freeze and makes 50 servings.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups water
  • 2 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 2 (3 ounce) packages strawberry flavored Jell-O mix
  • 1 (46 fluid ounce) can pineapple juice
  • 2/3 cup lemon juice
  • 1 quart orange juice
  • 2 (2 liter) bottles lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage

Steps

  1. Bring water, sugar, and strawberry flavored gelatin to a boil in a saucepan. Boil it for 3 minutes. Stir in pineapple juice, lemon juice, and orange juice.
  2. Divide mixture into 2 containers and freeze.
  3. Combine contents of 1 container frozen juice mixture with 1 bottle lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage in a punch bowl. Stir it until slushy. Repeat with remaing portions as needed.
