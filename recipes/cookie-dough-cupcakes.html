Cookie Dough Cupcakes

Description

This recipe is a standard cupcake that contains cookie dough on the inside of it. It takes about 2 hours to cook and will create two dozen cupcakes to enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/2 cup softened butter
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup mini chocolate chips
  • 1 box (18.25 ounces) yellow cake mix
  • 1 1/3 cups water
  • 1/3 cup canola oil
