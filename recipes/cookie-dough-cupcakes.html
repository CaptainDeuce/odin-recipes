Cookie Dough Cupcakes

Description

This recipe is a standard cupcake that contains cookie dough on the inside of it. It takes about 2 hours to cook and will create two dozen cupcakes to enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/2 cup softened butter
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup mini chocolate chips
  • 1 box (18.25 ounces) yellow cake mix
  • 1 1/3 cups water
  • 1/3 cup canola oil

Steps

  1. Whisk flour, baking soda, and sea salt in a bowl. Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer in a separate bowl until smooth. Add 1 egg and vanilla extract and beat until smooth. Mix in the flour mixture from earlier. Place in some chocolate chips and mix. Form the dough into small balls. Place on a baking sheet and freeze for 2 hours.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Line 24 muffin cups with paper liners.
  3. Lightly beat 3 eggs in a bowl with electric mixer. Add cake mix, water, and canola oil. Continue beating with mixer for 2 minutes. Spoon into prepared cupcake liners, filling each 2/3 full. Place a frozen cookie ball into each cupcake.
  4. Bake in the oven until a toothpick inserted into the cake portion comes clean, about 20 minutes. Cool in pans for 10 minutes before removing to cool on a plate.
